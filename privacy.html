MotorCamp — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 April 2026

Overview

MotorCamp is an off-grid camping and adventure mapping app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. MotorCamp is a client-side application — all data stays on your device.

Location Data

The app requests access to your device's GPS location to:

  • Center the map on your current position
  • Display your speed, heading, altitude, and lean angle on the ride dashboard
  • Calculate distances to campsites and services
  • Provide compass navigation to a chosen destination

Your location data is processed entirely on your device. It is never sent to our servers and is not stored beyond the current session. The app functions without location access — you can deny the permission and use the map manually.

Device Sensors

The app may access device motion sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope, compass) for the ride dashboard's lean angle and heading features. This sensor data is processed on-device only and is not stored or transmitted.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage, IndexedDB, Cache API):

  • Saved routes — route start/end/waypoints you explicitly save
  • Compass target — your chosen navigation destination
  • Map tile cache — cached map images for faster loading and offline use
  • POI cache — cached campsite and service data from OpenStreetMap
  • Weather cache — cached weather data from Open-Meteo

You can clear all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this app.

Third-Party Services

The app makes direct requests from your browser to these third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap (map tiles, search via Nominatim, POI data via Overpass API) — OSM Privacy Policy
  • Open-Meteo (weather forecasts, elevation data) — Open-Meteo Terms
  • OpenWeatherMap (weather overlay tiles) — OWM Privacy Policy
  • OSRM (route planning) — open-source routing service
  • Esri (satellite basemap tiles) — Esri Privacy Policy

These services may log your IP address and the coordinates/queries you request. We have no control over their data practices — please review their policies.

Cookies & Tracking

MotorCamp does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or any tracking technologies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar services.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. All data is stored locally on your device and under your control. To delete it, clear the app's site data in your browser or device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
